Hey, My self Manish Kumawat

About Me I am a dedicated and aspiring aerospace engineer, with a master's degree from the University of Texas-Arlington and a robust background in mechanical engineering from Swami Keshwanand Institute of Technology, India. My academic journey has endowed me with a deep blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on expertise. I am deeply passionate about innovation and am committed to achieving excellence in all my endeavors.
My professional experience includes working as a research assistant at the University of Texas at Arlington, where I specialized in advanced thermal cooling solutions, focusing particularly on metal foam single-phase liquid immersion cooling. Before this role, I contributed as an associate Mechanical engineer at AHA 3D Innovations Pvt. Ltd. And worked on STLA Brain as an Embedded systems Engineer at KPIT Technologies Ltd. I gained significant practical experience during my Mechanical Engineering internship at Bharat-Benz Pvt. Ltd. Notably, my projects such as the Aluminium Porous Foam Heat Transfer Study, the development of the Tesla Valvular conduit (Tesla valve), UAV System design, and Rocket Nozzle Design, highlight my capability to innovate and drive results in complex engineering challenges.
